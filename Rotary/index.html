Welcome to District 9212

Rotary is a global network of 1.2 million neighbors, friends, leaders, and like-minded problem-solvers who aim to create positive and lasting change in their communities and themselves.

Mission

We provide service to others, promote integrity, and advance world understanding, goodwill, and peace through our fellowship of business, professional, and community leaders.

Vision

Together we see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change – across the globe, in our communities, and in ourselves.

District Governor's Note

It is my pleasure to welcome you all to the Rotary District 9212 Conference, which will be held from the 25th to the 28th of April 2024. This conference promises to be a spectacular event that will bring together Rotary members from all corners of the district. The theme of this year’s conference is “We Are One,” a powerful message that emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration in our work as Rotarians. As members of this global organization, we all share a common goal of making a positive impact in our communities and the world at large.
